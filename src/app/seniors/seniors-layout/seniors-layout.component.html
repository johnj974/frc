Services Available

A range of services and supports are available to our senior citizens

Here to help

They can also avail of our laundry service including pre-arranged collection and drop off if required. Most importantly this service offers an opportunity for social interaction and mental and physical stimulation. For the older people in our community and beyond their visit to the Centre each week can be a lifeline, something to look forward to.
Social interaction is recognised as crucial to the well-being and health of the elderly. Our laundry, DIY service, hairdresser and chiropodist help in a practical way to make life a little easier and less stressful. This service is supported by funding from the HSE.
