About the Centre

FRC is at the heart of the community and serves the public.

The Centre comprises a large multi-purpose hall, meeting rooms, kitchen, laundry, DIY & grass cutting service, a day-care Centre for senior citizens and FRC Childcare. Located The ARC we have an AstroTurf , phase 1 of a planned multi-phase community sports facility. We also manage and maintain the Fair Green. We receive funding from the HSE to run our Senior Citizens day-care service. We are grateful for the grants received from Pobal, Dept. of Tourism and Sport, NEWKD and Kerry County Council. These grants have allowed us to improve our facilities and services.
