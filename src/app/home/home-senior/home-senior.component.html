Senior Citizens

Senior Citizens Committee offer a day care service every Tuesday.

New Senior Citizens are always welcome to join. Please contact us if you have any questions regarding the Senior Citizens service. Those who attend can enjoy a nutritious meal prepared on site by a qualified chef. We also provide the option of taking home a meal for the following day. Participants can engage in a programme of stimulating activities. Hairdressing and chiropody services are available at the Centre. Our senior citizens can look forward to planned social outings with their peers.
